Introducing The @media Rule

The @media rule lets you re-style content based on the size of the viewport screen.

Another example

Often, you'll see media queries used to re-size and re-design a full-page layout to a layout that looks good on a smaller screen. Screens have standard breakpoints. A full-size laptop screen is about 1200-1600px wide, while a phone screen may range from 300 to 800px, depending on the phone and whether the device is in landscape or portrait mode.
